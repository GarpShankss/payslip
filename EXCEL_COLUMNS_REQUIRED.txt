REQUIRED EXCEL COLUMNS FOR PAYSLIP GENERATOR
=============================================

✅ COLUMN NAMES ARE NOW CASE-INSENSITIVE!
You can use CAPS, lowercase, or mixed case - all will work!

EMPLOYEE INFORMATION:
- EMP_ID          (Employee ID)
- NAME            (Employee Name)
- DESIGNATION     (Job Title)
- UNIT_NAME       (Department/Unit)
- UAN_NO          (UAN Number)
- ESI_NO          (ESI Number)
- DOJ             (Date of Joining)
- BANK_AC         (Bank Account Number)
- IFSC_CODE       (IFSC Code)
- EMAIL           (Email Address)
- PHONE           (Phone Number)
- BASIC_DAYS      (Total Days in Month, default: 31)
- ACTUAL_DAYS     (Days Worked, default: 31)

SALARY - FIXED:
- FIXED_BASIC     (Fixed Basic Salary)
- FIXED_DA        (Fixed DA)
- FIXED_HRA       (Fixed HRA)
- FIXED_BONUS     (Fixed Bonus)
- FIXED_TOTAL     (Total Fixed Salary)

SALARY - EARNED:
- EARNED_BASIC    (Earned Basic Salary)
- EARNED_DA       (Earned DA)
- EARNED_HRA      (Earned HRA)
- EARNED_BONUS    (Earned Bonus)
- OTHER_ALLOWANCE (Other Allowances)
- EARNED_TOTAL    (Total Earned Salary)

DEDUCTIONS:
- PF              (Provident Fund)
- ESI             (ESI Deduction)
- PT              (Professional Tax)
- LWF             (Labour Welfare Fund)
- TOTAL_DEDUCTION (Total Deductions)

NET PAY:
- NET_PAY         (Net Salary After Deductions)

IMPORTANT NOTES:
1. ✅ Column names are CASE-INSENSITIVE (CAPS, lowercase, mixed - all work!)
2. ✅ Empty rows are automatically skipped
3. ✅ Rows without employee name are skipped
4. Use underscores (_) not spaces in column names
5. Example: EMP_ID, Emp_Id, emp_id - all work the same!
